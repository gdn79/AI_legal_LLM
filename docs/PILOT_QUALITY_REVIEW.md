# Pilot Quality Review

## Legal quality

- Plaintiff is identified correctly.
- Respondent is identified correctly.
- Signatory is identified correctly.
- Signatory authority is confirmed.
- Power of attorney is included when employee signatory is used.
- Claim amount is checked by lawyer.
- Pretrial procedure is reflected.
- Pretension proof exists.
- Claim copy proof exists.
- Prayer for relief is reviewed by lawyer.
- RAG sources open and can be checked.
- Disputed conclusions are marked with warnings.

## Technical quality

- Case passes workflow without developer intervention.
- UI does not block the user without reason.
- Errors are understandable.
- Export contains 12 sections.
- Audit log is complete.
- Pilot feedback can be created and triaged.
- Pilot metrics are collected and exportable.

## AI / RAG quality

- AI does not invent facts.
- AI does not invent legal sources.
- AI does not invent powers of attorney.
- AI marks uncertainty.
- RAG returns relevant sources for typical scenarios.
- Missing source leads to warning.
- Lawyer can verify the basis quickly.
